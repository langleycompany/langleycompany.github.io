Get Started

First things first, paste this into your head.

<link href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/jordanreger/airframe/airframe.css" rel="stylesheet">

Next (and last), use divs named according to what you want, and what column you're on.

Example:

<div class="two-one">Left</div>
<div class="two-two">Right</div>

That's it. Seriously.
