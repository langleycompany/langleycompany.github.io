19. Flashback

[June 24, 2019 - June 25, 2019 at like 2AM]

SpaceX is getting ready to launch STP-2 on their Falcon Heavy rocket (the one that has 2 landing boosters). We made the trip down to Daytona Beach specifically for this event, so I was as ready as I would ever be. We toured Kennedy Space Center (for the second time) until around 10PM. The launch was supposed to happen at around 11:30PM, but it was pushed back until around 2AM the next day. During this time, I slept on a cooler outside the car, trying to stay awake and not get bitten by mosquitoes.





My family was in the car, turning the air conditioning on for a bit, then off, trying to stay cool. Eventually, it launched and I almost had a heart attack because I was so excited (and quite tired). As an aspiring aerospace engineer, that day will probably stick with me for the rest of my life.
